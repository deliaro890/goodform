Design and build of rooftop trackway for artist Ian Thuillier's project Twenty4.

Liberty Hall, Dublin 1.

In the heart of Dublin, the acclaimed Irish design firm goodform was tasked with a unique project for a renowned artist: the creation of a bespoke rooftop trackway that would serve as both a personal sanctuary and an inspiring creative space. The vision was to seamlessly blend functionality with artistic flair, crafting a serene environment where the artist could jog, meditate, and find inspiration amidst panoramic views of the city. Utilizing weather-resistant composite decking and ambient lighting, the trackway was adorned with planters of native Irish flora and strategically placed seating areas. The centerpiece, a sleek pergola, provided both shade and an open-air studio atmosphere, embodying goodform's commitment to innovative, sustainable design and enhancing the artist's daily life and creative process.
