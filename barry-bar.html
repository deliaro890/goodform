Design team: goodform

Design and build of restaurant interior.

Barry's Bar, Dublin 4.

In the vibrant city of Dublin, the design team at GoodForm took on the ambitious task of creating a wooden ceiling for a new, upscale restaurant. The vision was to craft a ceiling that would not only enhance the restaurant's aesthetic appeal but also create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Using locally sourced oak, renowned for its durability and rich texture, the artisans meticulously crafted each beam, ensuring that the natural grains and knots were prominently featured. The ceiling design incorporated intricate lattice patterns that drew inspiration from traditional Irish motifs, seamlessly blending with the restaurant's modern decor. Subtle, recessed lighting was installed to highlight the wood's natural beauty, casting a gentle glow that added to the cozy ambiance. The result was a stunning wooden ceiling that served as both a centerpiece and a testament to GoodForm's dedication to craftsmanship and innovative design.
