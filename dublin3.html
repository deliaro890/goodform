goodform

construction + design practice

Design team: Architecture Republic.

Facade retention, demolition, re-build and interior furniture design.

Private house, Dublin 3.

GoodForm was tasked with the ambitious project of rebuilding a beloved family home from its foundations while infusing it with bespoke interior furniture design. The journey began with the careful deconstruction of the old structure, preserving cherished elements like the original stone fireplace and hand-carved wooden beams. As the new walls rose, the team at GoodForm envisioned a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity, crafting custom furniture that echoed the house’s rustic heritage yet offered contemporary comfort. Locally sourced oak and ash were transformed into elegant, functional pieces, from a grand dining table perfect for family feasts to cozy, upholstered window seats ideal for quiet contemplation. Each piece was designed to tell a story, connecting the home’s storied past with its vibrant future. The result was a stunning, revitalized dwelling that exuded warmth and character, a testament to GoodForm's mastery in creating spaces where old-world charm and modern sophistication coalesce beautifully.
