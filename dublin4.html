Design team: goodform

Facade improvement, complete renovation and furniture design.

Private house, Dublin 4.

In the prestigious neighborhood of Dublin 4, goodform was commissioned to undertake a remarkable transformation: the facade improvement, complete renovation, and bespoke furniture design of a stately yet aging house. The vision was to rejuvenate the home's exterior with a sophisticated blend of classic and contemporary elements, preserving its historic charm while introducing modern elegance. The facade was meticulously restored, featuring sleek new windows and a fresh limestone finish that radiated timeless appeal. Inside, the renovation unveiled a spacious, open-plan layout bathed in natural light, with skylights and glass doors connecting the interior to lush garden views. goodform's artisans crafted custom furniture that married functionality with style, from an exquisite walnut dining table that became the heart of family gatherings to tailored bookshelves that framed cozy reading nooks. Each piece was thoughtfully designed to complement the home's refined aesthetic. The result was a beautifully revitalized residence, seamlessly blending heritage and innovation, embodying the essence of Dublin 4's architectural elegance.
