Interior furniture design and installation.

Private House, County Wicklow.

Nestled in the tranquil landscapes of Wicklow, the Irish design firm GoodForm embarked on a bespoke interior furniture project for a charming countryside house, aiming to marry rustic charm with modern elegance. The design journey began with a deep dive into the homeowner's lifestyle and aesthetic preferences, leading to the creation of custom pieces that reflected both functionality and artistry. Locally sourced oak and ash were transformed into timeless furniture, each piece meticulously crafted to highlight the natural grains and textures of the wood. From a grand farmhouse dining table that became the heart of family gatherings to cozy window seats perfect for quiet moments of reflection, every element was thoughtfully designed to enhance the warmth and comfort of the home.
