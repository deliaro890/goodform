Private House, County Kerry.

In the heart of County Kerry, an enchanting private house, steeped in history and charm, awaited a much-needed restoration. Entrusted to goodform, a renowned Irish design firm, the project aimed to breathe new life into the aged stone walls and weathered timbers while preserving the home’s authentic character. The journey commenced with a deep dive into the house’s past, respecting its architectural nuances and storied legacy. Expert artisans painstakingly restored original features, from the intricate moldings to the grand staircase, blending them seamlessly with modern comforts. Bespoke furnishings were crafted to echo the house's historical era, while discreetly incorporating contemporary amenities. The restored home emerged as a harmonious blend of old and new, its rustic elegance shining through every meticulously restored detail. It stood as a testament to goodform's commitment to heritage and innovation, ready to welcome future generations into its warm, storied embrace.
